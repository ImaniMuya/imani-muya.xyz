About me
Hi, I'm Imani Muya. I'm equal parts creative and analytical. I'm invigorated by interesting problems and enjoy brainstorming solutions. I'm passionate about people and technology, and I love working with teams and problem solving.
I love telling stories.
I enjoy using storytelling as a tool that to remove barriers between people of different backgrounds. My hope is that through my storytelling I can give voices to the sides that are told less often. I have found a passion for documentary storytelling where I get to go out in the world and find amazing people. I have also found a love for developing my own characters and stories, making them feel real and relatable, and watching them interact with a world that I create for them.
I make games.
An intersection of creativity and logic that I have had the privilege of diving into is game development. It seems a little strange because I didn't grow up playing video games, but in college, I've spent many hours making them. I have competed in the Computer Science and Engineering department’s gamejam for the past 3 years. I have made games from platformers to puzzles, and even a tower defense. Working alongside incredibly talented people has made it possible for me to learn and create things that I would not have been able to do on my own.
I've dabbled in design.
Through my coursework, I've gotten to design things like web animations and other visuals. I intend to keep learning and improving my design skills throughout every project I do, design-related or not.

Nurk

Documentary about a professional drone racer. This documentary took a full semester, and taught me how to weave a story together with interviews, archival footage, and b-roll.
